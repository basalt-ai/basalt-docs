# Deploy your chain

Deploying your chain in the Basalt dashboard will allow you to monitor your chain in your own applications.

## Deploy to production

Once you've created your chain and tested it thoroughly in the playground, you can deploy it to production.

### Go to your chain Overview

Go to your chain Overview and click the "slug" button in the deployment section.

<Image src={chainHomepage} alt="Image alt" />

### Follow the steps to deploy

A modal will open with the steps to deploy your chain. Follow the steps to deploy your chain.
Enter a unique identifier for your chain. This will be used to identify your chain in the API.

<Image src={chainSlugSetting} alt="Image alt" />

Verify you have your API key. If you don't have it, you can create one in the your Settings.

<div className="not-prose">
  <Button href="/authentication#get-your-api-key" variant="text" arrow="right">
    <>Create an API key</>
  </Button>
</div>

<Note>
  Choose your slug carefully as it must be unique within your workspace and cannot be modified after deployment.
</Note>

You did it! Your chain is now deployed and you can now push traces & logs to it.. 

<div className="not-prose">

    <>Monitor your chain</>
  </Button>
</div>
