# Deploy your prompt

Deploying your prompt in the Basalt dashboard will allow you to use your prompt in your own applications.

## Deploy to production

Once you've created your prompt and tested it thoroughly in the playground, you can deploy it to production.


### Go to your prompt Overview

Go to your prompt Overview and click the "Deploy" button in the deployment section.

<Image src={deployButton} alt="Image alt" />

### Follow the steps to deploy

A modal will open with the steps to deploy your prompt. Follow the steps to deploy your prompt.

<Image src={deployKeyStep} alt="Image alt" />

Verify you have your API key. If you don't have it, you can create one in the your Settings.

<div className="not-prose">
  <Button href="/authentication#get-your-api-key" variant="text" arrow="right">
    <>Create an API key</>
  </Button>
</div>

<Image src={deploySlugStep} alt="Image alt" />

Enter a unique identifier for your prompt. This will be used to identify your prompt in the API.

<Note>
  Choose your slug carefully as it must be unique within your workspace and cannot be modified after deployment.
</Note>

<Image src={deployVersionStep} alt="Image alt" />

Chose the version of your prompt you want to deploy as "production" version.

<Image src={deploySdkStep} alt="Image alt" />

You did it! Your prompt is now deployed and you can now get it from the API.
